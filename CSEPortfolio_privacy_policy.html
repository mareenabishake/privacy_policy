Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to CSE Portfolio ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Email address for authentication purposes only.
  • Portfolio Data: Stock symbols, transaction records, and portfolio information that you voluntarily enter into the app.

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal financial account details or banking information
  • National Identity Card (NIC) numbers or government IDs
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or personal files
  • Browsing history outside our app

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide portfolio tracking functionality
  • Display your stock holdings and transaction history
  • Calculate portfolio performance metrics

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Analyze your data for profiling

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • We use secure, reputable cloud infrastructure
  • Access to data is strictly limited and monitored
  • Regular security audits are performed

Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Stock price data providers for real-time market information
  • Authentication services for secure login

These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them. We do not share your personal portfolio data with these services beyond what is necessary for functionality.

Your Rights

Under the Personal Data Protection Act of Sri Lanka and applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Authority of Sri Lanka

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to delete such information in accordance with applicable Sri Lankan laws.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the app or our support channels.
